Together, we’ll find a way forward

When mental health challenges feel overwhelming, expert guidance can make all the difference.

As a board-certified psychiatrist and a therapist, I offer comprehensive mental health care that integrates medication management and evidence-based psychotherapy.

My clinical experience spans multiple settings within the mental health system: Inpatient units, outpatient clinics, consultation-liaison services, and community mental health centers.

This diverse background gives me a top-down understanding of the American mental health system.

If you are feeling lost, we’ll find a path forward.

Personalized Psychiatry for Children & Adults

Connection

Your healing journey begins with connection.

I believe in building collaborative partnerships with my patients, fostering trust that allows us to unlock their innate capacity for healing and growth.

Medication may not be required for you to become the healthiest version of yourself.

Guidance

As a medical doctor and a therapist, I have a top-down view of the American mental healthcare system.

I provide informed guidance through complex diagnostic and systemic bottlenecks.

Working with a doctor ensures your mental healthcare needs are properly communicated to your primary care and specialist teams.

Custom Care

While working with all of my patients, I take the time to understand their unique experiences and personality.

I also offer concierge services for patients seeking a closer doctor-patient relationship.
